### Phase 2: Critical Fixes (Just Implemented) ✅

#### 1. **Blocked Pagination from Indexing** (CRITICAL FIX)
**Problem**: Pagination pages like `/blog/p/11` were ranking at position **1.5**, competing with main content and diluting ranking signals.

**Solution Implemented**:
- Updated `robots.js` to block all pagination patterns:
  - `/blog/p/*` - Main blog pagination
  - `/blog/tp/*` - Tool pagination  
  - `*/p/1`, `*/p/2`, etc. - Page number duplicates
  
- Added `X-Robots-Tag: noindex` header in `middleware.js` for all pagination URLs

**Expected Impact**:
- Remove 20+ duplicate pagination pages from Google index
- Consolidate ranking signals to main content pages
- Improve crawl budget efficiency
- Eliminate duplicate content issues
