## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Quality > Quantity:** 10 high-quality backlinks beat 100 low-quality ones
2. **Diversify:** Get links from different types of sites
3. **Natural Anchor Text:** Use varied, natural anchor text
4. **Be Patient:** Link building takes time (3-6 months)
5. **Track Everything:** Use spreadsheet to track submissions
6. **Follow Up:** Don't be afraid to follow up on outreach
7. **Provide Value:** Always offer value in exchange for links
